Units of Measurement

Block vs. Inline Elements

Block elements take up 100% of the space, doesn't share line with other 
elements, far left or far right

Inline Elements allow other elements to share the same line

CSS Selectors
Specificity: a score or rank that will determine which style declaration will be
applied 

Pseudo-classes allow you to change the appearance of elements when a user
is interacting with them
