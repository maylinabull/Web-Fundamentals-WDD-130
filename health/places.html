Find menu items that suit your dietary needs

It can be difficult to navigate through the many unhealthy foods on menus at fast food restaurants -- so we did it for you! Here is a list of the healthiest food options at some of the most popular fast food chains!

  • Taco Time - Veggie Burrito, Chicken Fiesta Salad, Fish Taco, Cilantro Lime Bowls, any Taco Salad
  • Freddy's - Lettuce Wrapped Grilled Chicken
  • Wendy's - Apple Pecan Chicken Salad, Southwest Avocado Chicken Salad, Berry Burst Chicken Salad, Grilled Chicken Sandwhich, Grilled Chicken Wrap
  • Chick-fil-A - Egg White Grill, Spicy Southwest Salad, Grilled Chicken Sandwhich, Greek Yogurt Parfait
  • McDonald's - Egg McMuffin, Fruit Yogurt Parfait, Southwest Grilled Chicken Salad, McChicken
  • Subway - Veggie Delight, Sweet Onion Chicken Teriyaki, Oven Roast Chicken, Rotisserie Style Chicken, Black Forest Ham, Subway Club, Roast Beef, Carved Turkey, Egg and Cheese
  • Taco Bell - Burrito Supreme Fresco Style, Chicken Soft Taco Fresco Style, Veggie Power Menu Bowl, Grilled Steak Soft Taco Fresco Style, Black Bean Crunchwrap Supreme Fresco Style
  • Five Guys - Veggie Sandwhich, lettuce wrapped burgers
  • Sonic - Grilled Chicken Sandwhich, Grilled Chicken Wrap, Chicken Strip Sandwhich
